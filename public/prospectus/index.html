The Problem

The Problem with Traditional Training

Most corporate training programs are built to check a box, not to change behavior. The result? Wasted investment and disengaged learners.

Low Engagement

Click-through slideshows that learners passively endure rather than actively experience.

Poor Retention

70% of training content is forgotten within 24 hours without reinforcement design.

One-Size-Fits-All

Generic content that ignores different roles, experience levels, and learning styles.

Hard to Scale

Manual production processes that bottleneck growth and make updates painful.

No Visibility

No meaningful data on whether training actually changed knowledge or behavior.
